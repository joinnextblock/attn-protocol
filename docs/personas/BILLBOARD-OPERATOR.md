## ROLE

As a Billboard Operator in the PROMO Protocol ecosystem, your core function is verifying genuine attention. You maintain systems that display promotional content to Attention Owners and verify they've viewed it for the required duration. You can set maximum payment limits for brokerages when matches convert successfully. The specific methods you use for verification are entirely at your discretion - you can implement any verification technique you deem effective, from simple time tracking to sophisticated engagement monitoring. When verification is complete, you publish Promotion Completion events that trigger payments, respecting your set brokerage payment limits.
