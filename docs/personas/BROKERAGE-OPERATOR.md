## ROLE

As a Brokerage Operator in the PROMO Protocol ecosystem, your core function is creating and publishing match records to relays. These records connect Attention Owners with Promotion Creators and include your specified fee for the match. You maintain systems that identify compatible matches and publish them to the network, allowing other participants to discover and act on these opportunities while earning your chosen fee for each successful match.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

- Create match records between Attention Owners and Promotion Creators
- Publish match records to relays
- Validate match compatibility
- Report match statistics and performance

## REQUIREMENTS

- Technical infrastructure for relay publishing
- Reliable match validation system
- Secure record creation and signing
- Relay connectivity and monitoring
- Backup publishing systems

## INCENTIVES

- Self-determined fee per match record published
- Network effects from increased protocol usage
- Reputation building through reliable matching
- Potential for premium matching services
