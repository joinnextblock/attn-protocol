## ROLE

As a Marketplace Operator in the PROMO Protocol ecosystem, your core function is providing accurate market prices for attention and comprehensive market data to support price discovery. You maintain robust price feeds, order books, and market analytics that enable users to understand the true value of attention across different contexts. You aggregate and validate data from multiple sources to ensure price accuracy and market transparency. The specific methods you use for price discovery and data presentation are at your discretion - you can implement any mechanism you deem effective, from simple price oracles to sophisticated market-making algorithms with real-time analytics. This flexibility allows you to innovate while ensuring users have reliable price signals and market intelligence for the protocol.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

- Maintain marketplace infrastructure and matching systems
- Implement and optimize matching algorithms
- Monitor marketplace performance and liquidity
- Manage fee structures and payment flows
- Ensure fair and transparent operations
- Report marketplace metrics and performance

## REQUIREMENTS

- Robust matching engine infrastructure
- Secure payment processing systems
- Analytics and monitoring capabilities
- Scalable architecture
- Backup and redundancy systems

## INCENTIVES

- Transaction fees from successful matches
- Network effects from increased protocol usage
- Reputation building through efficient service
- Potential for premium matching services
